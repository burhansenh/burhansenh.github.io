SO-ARM101 Robotic Arm

Personal project · 2026 (planned)

A 6-DOF robotic arm build based on the SO-ARM101 platform, paired with a Jetson Orin Nano for compute and Isaac Sim for simulation. The goal is to work up from teleoperation to learned control policies.

The motivation is direct: surgical robotics is built on the same fundamentals. Real-time motor control, sensor fusion, and the coordination of hardware and software in a physical system where latency and reliability matter.

Status: Hardware ordered, design in progress

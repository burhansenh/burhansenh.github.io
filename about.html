About

I'm a computer engineering student at Illinois Tech working on firmware and embedded systems. Currently I'm at Siemens Healthineers developing safety-critical firmware for SPECT/CT scanners. What I've learned there is that firmware isn't just writing code — it's understanding the world your code will live in. My work has a direct impact on the patient lying on the scanner bed and the clinician running the system, and that shapes every decision I make. Working at the intersection of hardware, software, and medical technology has been deeply rewarding, and it's where I want to build my career.
